## STM32 MIPI-DSI测试

目标：STM32驱动HSD088IPW1屏幕

难点：HSD088IPW1采用MIPI协议，且接口规范不指定，至指定协议

目标屏幕：[8.8寸瀚彩 HSD088IPW1](https://detail.tmall.com/item.htm?spm=a230r.1.14.25.7554793cetRvJc&id=620557899761&ns=1&abbucket=5)
